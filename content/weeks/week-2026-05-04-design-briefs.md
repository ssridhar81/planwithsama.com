## BRIEF 2 — WEDNESDAY REEL
**Tool:** Seedance (background video) + CapCut (text assembly)
**Estimated time:** 30 minutes
**Complexity:** Medium

### Seedance Prompt

```
Generate a 25-second ambient background video. 9:16 vertical format (1080x1920px).

Scene: A warm, lived-in living room at dusk. Late evening light — approximately 8pm. The room is softly lit by a single floor or table lamp, casting a golden-amber pool of light. The scene is still, slightly cluttered in a human way: a half-finished cup of tea on a coffee table, a phone face-up on a cushion nearby, keys and unopened mail in the background, a child's drawing and a planner with a pen resting on the cover visible on a side surface. No people. No animals. No movement except: very slow, almost imperceptible dust motes drifting through the lamp light, and a gradual dimming through the window as the last of the daylight fades.

Mood: Quiet exhaustion. Real life. The beautiful weight of an ordinary evening. Not sad — just honest. Like the moment before the second shift truly begins.

Colour palette: Warm amber and honey tones from lamp glow. Deep, slightly cool shadows. The window shows a navy-indigo dusk sky. No harsh whites. No cool office tones. The room should feel lived-in, not staged.

Camera: Static or near-static. Very slow push in toward the coffee table scene — no more than 5% of travel over the full 25 seconds. Cinematic depth of field. Slight film grain texture welcome.

Lighting: Single warm light source (lamp). Practical interior lighting only. The golden-amber cast over everything. Window providing ambient indigo-dusk contrast.

Do not include: people, faces, hands, text, logos, pets, TV screens, or any motion other than the ambient drift and window light change described above.

Duration: 25 seconds, loopable preferred.
Style reference: The visual language of a Nils Frahm or Ólafur Arnalds music video — melancholic, intimate, real.
```

### CapCut Text Overlay Spec

All text: centred, over the Seedance video.
Default font: DM Sans Light or Playfair Display (as noted per line).
Default colour: White #FFFFFF with soft black text shadow (opacity 40%, blur 8px) for readability over warm backgrounds.
Text animation: Fade in — 0.3s ease in, 0.2s fade out between lines.
All text: centred horizontally, vertically positioned at approximately 40% from top (above centreline).

| Timing | Text | Font | Size | Notes |
|--------|------|------|------|-------|
| 0:00–0:02 | You use AI at work every day. | DM Sans Light | 36px | Fade in |
| 0:02–0:04 | Emails handled. | DM Sans Light | 34px | Slightly smaller — list rhythm |
| 0:04–0:05.5 | Meetings summarised. | DM Sans Light | 34px | Same rhythm |
| 0:05.5–0:07 | Scheduling sorted. | DM Sans Light | 34px | Same rhythm |
| 0:07–0:09.5 | And then you come home. | Playfair Display italic | 38px | Tone shift — serif for emotion |
| 0:09.5–0:12 | And start the second shift. | Playfair Display italic | 38px | Pause before next section |
| 0:12–0:13 | Manually. | DM Sans Light | 32px | Short, punchy, single word |
| 0:13–0:14 | Alone. | DM Sans Light | 32px | Single word — leave it to breathe |
| 0:14–0:15 | The dentist. | DM Sans Light | 30px | List rhythm — faster now |
| 0:15–0:16 | The school form. | DM Sans Light | 30px | Same |
| 0:16–0:17.5 | The birthday you nearly forgot. | DM Sans Light | 30px | Slightly longer — pause |
| 0:17.5–0:18.5 | The prescription. | DM Sans Light | 30px | Back to rhythm |
| 0:18.5–0:19.5 | The fridge. | DM Sans Light | 30px | Ends the list |
| 0:19.5–0:22 | All of it — in your head. | Playfair Display italic | 38px | Emotional peak of reel |
| 0:22–0:25 | Nobody gave you tools for this part. | DM Sans Light | 34px | Fade slower — 0.5s hold |
| 0:25–0:29 | You were always managing two jobs. It's time the second one got the same support. | DM Sans Light | 30px | Split to 2 lines — closing statement |
| 0:29–0:31 | [Endcard — see below] | — | — | Fade video to warm overlay |

### Endcard spec
- Duration: 2 seconds (0:29–0:31)
- Background: Fade the video to a semi-opaque Clay pale overlay (#F5EBE0 at 80% opacity) over the last video frame
- Centre top: "Sama" in Playfair Display, Clay #C4855A, 48px
- Centre: planwithsama.com in DM Sans Light, Ink, 28px
- Centre bottom: @planwithsama in DM Sans Light, Muted #8C8178, 24px
- No border, no pill — clean minimal card

### Music direction
- Mood: Melancholic but not heavy. The feeling of something ending and something better beginning.
- Tempo: Very slow, 60–70 BPM. Minimal beats. Space between notes is as important as the notes.
- Reference artists: Nils Frahm (especially "Says" or "All Melody"), Ólafur Arnalds ("Near Light"), Ludovico Einaudi ("Experience")
- Instrumentation: Solo piano, or piano with sparse warm ambient electronics. No vocals. No drums.
- Volume: Low — the silence between text lines must land. Keep music at 20–30% of max so text pacing carries.
- Where to find: CapCut's royalty-free library under "Cinematic / Ambient" — filter by slow tempo. Alternatively: Epidemic Sound under "Piano / Melancholy" or "Minimal Electronic."
- Total duration needed: 31-second loop

## BRIEF 4 — SUNDAY GRAPHIC
**Tool:** Claude Design
**Estimated time:** 5 minutes
**Complexity:** Simple

### Claude Design Prompt

```
Create a single square Instagram graphic (1080x1080px) for Sama — an AI planning platform for women managing career, home, health, family and self.

BRAND DESIGN SYSTEM
- Wordmark: "Sama" is NOT shown on this graphic (Sunday graphic is an exception to the standard carousel rule)
- Background: Dusk pale #F0EDF5 — full bleed, no texture, no gradient
- Primary text: Clay #C4855A (question) and Muted #8C8178 (supporting elements)
- Emphasis: Playfair Display italic
- Body: DM Sans light

LAYOUT — CENTRED, MINIMAL
The graphic has three elements only, all centred horizontally:

TOP (approximately 25% from top of canvas):
Eyebrow text in DM Sans light, Muted #8C8178, very small, letter-spaced, all caps:
"SUNDAY REFLECTION · @planwithsama"

CENTRE (vertically centred on canvas):
Main question in Playfair Display italic, Clay #C4855A, large:
"What would you let go of —"
"if you had a system to hold it?"
(Two lines, centred. This is the hero element. Give it generous space. The question mark should feel like an invitation, not a demand.)

BOTTOM (approximately 75% from top — above lower quarter):
Supporting line in DM Sans light, Muted #8C8178, small:
"Leave your answer below. We read every one."

Handle at very bottom (DM Sans light, Muted, small):
"@planwithsama"

VISUAL NOTES
- No Sama wordmark on this graphic
- No slide number, no dot navigation, no divider line, no CTA pill
- No decorative elements beyond the faint corner circle accent (Clay at 6% opacity, bottom right)
- Generous whitespace — the question needs room to breathe
- The dusk pale #F0EDF5 background is the mood: quiet, introspective, Sunday evening
- Total text on the canvas: 4 short lines. Nothing else.
```

### Visual notes
- This graphic deliberately breaks the standard carousel template — no wordmark, no navigation, no structure. The emptiness is the point.
- The question "What would you let go of — if you had a system to hold it?" is the most personal, vulnerable copy of the week. The design must honour that.
- Dusk pale #F0EDF5 is the correct background — do not substitute Warm #F9F4EE (too neutral) or Clay pale #F5EBE0 (too warm).
- If anything feels decorative or busy, remove it. Minimal is correct.
