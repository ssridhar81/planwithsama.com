### Post

Here's what I keep noticing though.

The same women who are leading AI transformation projects — building the business cases, training the teams, implementing the tools — come home and manage a completely separate, equally complex operation entirely in their heads.

The school schedule. The appointments. The family admin. The health routines. The household planning that runs constantly in the background, regardless of what's on the work calendar.

Enterprise AI has never once touched any of that.

The gap is stark. We've been very good at optimising the 9-to-5. Nobody optimised what comes after it. Nobody built the intelligent, connected planning system for the rest of a woman's life.

That's what Sama is.

It started as a personal problem — because I was one of those women. Using every AI tool professionally. Running everything else manually. The imbalance was too obvious to ignore.

Sama is an AI-assisted planning platform for women managing career, home, health, family and self simultaneously. Not a calendar app. Not another productivity tool. A proper AI co-pilot for the whole of your life.

The organisations investing in AI for their workforce are missing half the picture. A woman who is calmer, more organised and less cognitively overloaded outside work doesn't just feel better. She performs better.

Is anyone else seeing this gap in the AI tools conversation?
